Welcome- Diamond's CPA Packages for Less

Diamond’s vision is to empower people around the world to advance their careers through a lifelong partnership of superior professional education.


We are proud of our rich history and track record of growth spanning more than 60 years. Our national presence includes more than 900 partners—including accounting firms, societies, corporations, government agencies and universities.


Diamond’s Packages for Less only sells products that have received accreditation by the Accrediting Council for Continuing Education & Training (ACCET). ACCET is a U.S. Department of Education nationally recognized agency that promotes quality-oriented education and training through the establishment of standards and an accreditation process focused on integrity.


Our national partners in over 30 states share a commitment to helping candidates achieve their CPA credential and further the advancement of their careers. We serve the Big 4 global accounting firms and maintain strong relationships with 99 of the top 100 accounting firms in the United States.


With a focus on delivering the most current content and software functionality that mirrors the exam in every way, Diamond’s CPA Packages for Less helps our students attain the CPA Credential.


At Diamond’s, every package we offer helps aspiring accounting professionals maintain a competitive edge!
